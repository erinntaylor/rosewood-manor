WELCOME TO ROSEWOOD MANOR

Where Gilded Age elegance

Experience Rosewood Manor, where Gilded Age elegence meets modern day comfort. Located on Newport's famed Cliff Walk overlooking the Atlantic ocean, we offer unparalleled serenity. Spend your days exploring all Newport has to offer, or spend your days lounging in this extravagent oasis. If you're lucky, you might make friends with one or two of the spirits that roam the halls of this historic mansion.
